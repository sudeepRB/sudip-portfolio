Love Battery Project

Role

Modeling, Scripting, Animation, Video Editing

Key Tools Used

Adobe Photoshop Autodesk Maya Premiere Pro

Storyline

"Love Battery" is a heartwarming 3D animation set in a toy-filled room, where a robot and a doll powered by batteries share a brief, playful connection. When the doll's battery runs out, the robot selflessly gives up its own power to revive her, embodying themes of sacrifice and companionship.
